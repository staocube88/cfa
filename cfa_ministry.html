Our Ministry

The main focus of our Ministry is to reach out to the rich and the needy and alternately our community with the aim of winning souls for the kingdom, according to Acts 6. We do this through Counselling, different kinds of ministrations, monthly evangelism where we minister to the people in Medicare, food and relief materials, and more importantly feeding their spiritual being with the word of God.

Youth Alive For Christ

The acronym YAFC stands for Youth Alive For Christ. YAFC is Singles Network is committed to raising people with a deep understanding of Kingdom principles because your youth is the proper time to set the foundation and build the framework of your life. Whether you think you are young or not, your single days are days for building yourself. These are the days of building your vision. This is the time to discover & develop yourself. At YAFC Events, you get to meet exciting people who are also passionate about God and His kingdom. Participate in games, exhibition, entertainment, competitions and learn how to live a healthy & productive life that is well pleasing unto God. You also get to enjoy meals and refreshment as you have fun with friends.
